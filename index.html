USGS Stream Gauge Locations

This map displays USGS stream gauge locations where the National Water Model assimilates streamflow observations. By clicking on points on the map you can obtain metadata for stream gauge locations including USGS site codes and associated NWM Reach ids. It uses the following web service: https://mapservices.weather.noaa.gov/static/rest/services/nws_reference_maps/USGS_Stream_Gauges/MapServer
